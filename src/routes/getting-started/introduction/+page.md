# Welcome to AceRex Factions

AceRex Factions is a competitive Minecraft survival server built around territory control, raiding, and faction warfare. Team up with friends, claim land, build your base, and fight for dominance. Or go solo and prove you don't need anyone.

<br />

---

# What is Factions?

Factions is a game mode where players form teams, claim chunks of land to protect their builds and storage, and compete to become the most powerful faction on the server. Your claimed territory is yours, enemies can't break blocks or open chests inside it. But if your faction loses enough power (by dying), your land becomes vulnerable and can be seized.

:::note
Factions != Survival! What does this mean? Factions is not the same as playing normal minecrat survival mode or SMP servers. Forget everything you know about survival! PvP is on everywhere outside of spawn, and your items drop on death!
:::

# Joining the Server

1. Launch Minecraft and add `play.acerex.net` to your server list.
2. Connect and spawn at the **main hub**.
3. Read the holograms around spawn, they cover server specific features and any active events.
4. Type `/rules` to review the full ruleset before playing.

# Your First Steps

**At spawn (safe zone — no PvP):**
- Claim your starter kit with `/kit starter`.
- Check your balance with `/balance`.
- Browse the player shop with `/shop`.

**Heading out:**
- Spawn is surrounded by a ***warzone***. PvP is on and the land is unclaimed. Move through it quickly.
- Travel far into the ***wilderness*** before settling. The further from spawn, the safer you are from early raiders.
- Once you've found a spot, use `/f create <name>` to start your faction, then `/f claim` to protect your land.

**Joining a faction instead:**
- Type `/f list` to browse active factions, or ask in chat if anyone is recruiting.
- Use `/f show <factionname>` to check their power and member count.
- Accept an invite with `/f join <factionname>`.

# Key Things to Know

**[Power](\gameplay/power)** — Every player has a power level that determines how much land their faction can claim. You lose power on death, and if your faction's power drops below its land count, enemies can steal your territory. Keep your power high; don't die carelessly.

**[Claiming Land](\gameplay/claiming-land)** — Claimed chunks (16×16 columns from bedrock to sky) protect everything inside them. Enemies can't build, break, or loot inside your claims as long as your power holds.

**[The Warzone](\gameplay/the-warzone)** — The ring around spawn has no faction claims and full PvP. Don't store anything valuable there.

**[Allies vs Enemies](\gameplay/relationships)** — You can set relationships with other factions. Allies can't PvP each other and can access your territory, so only ally factions you genuinely trust.

# Useful Starting Commands

| Command | What it does |
|---|---|
| `/f create <name>` | Create a new faction |
| `/f join <name>` | Join a faction you've been invited to |
| `/f claim` | Claim the chunk you're standing in |
| `/f sethome` | Set your faction's home (do this inside claimed land) |
| `/f home` | Teleport to your faction home |
| `/f map` | See nearby chunk ownership |
| `/f show [name]` | View a faction's stats |
| `/f help` | Full list of faction commands |

For a complete command reference, see the [Commands](commands) page.
